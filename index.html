About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Software Engineering at the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT). I received my PhD from the University of Texas at San Antonio in 2021. My research interests are in software engineering, program analysis, privacy, and natural language processing, with an emphasis on detecting privacy leaks on Android mobile applications through program analysis and machine learning.
